### job4 18 months

It seems company culture has reached a turning point with regards to AI (LLM) use.
No longer are we just given all the tools and encouraged to adopt it,
it's an all in bet on AI with adoption mandates.

What does that mean?
First a project that has to be primarily driven through ai "vibecoding",
and coming up, trying to orchestrate agents to do the work for you
by prompting through jira tickets, and not driving the session yourself.

Besides adopting AI, there was work in cleaning up migrations,
actually adopting new features that were added but not widely used,
wiring up more context in errors, logs, tags, events, UIs, etc.
Also argocd and atlantis/terraform perf improvements.

The big piece I was working on was with [Gitops Promoter](https://github.com/argoproj-labs/gitops-promoter)
and some custom deployment gates to go with it.
With that, our team now has a proper automatic deployment across environments.
